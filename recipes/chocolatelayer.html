chocolate layer cake

description

a rich, chocolatey explosion!

nuts are completely optional but make a great garnish. :)

ingredients

  • cocoa powder
  • flour
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • white sugar
  • canola oil
  • eggs
  • milk
  • vanilla extract

steps

  1. preheat the oven.325 fahrenheit should be perfect. grease a 9x13 baking pan; dust the pan with 2 tsp cocoa powder or as desired/needed.
  2. mix the ingredients. mix flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. mix sugar and canola oil. beat eggs, milk, and vanilla extract in gradually; then beat in dry mixture.
  3. pour into baking pan.
  4. bake. bake in the preheated oven for about 40 minutes.
